My Story

Hi, I'm Lyn - a baking lover from Saigon, VN.

Honestly, I'm not a professional baker. On the contrary, I am extremely clumsy. More detail, I used to be person who are not really care about cooking & baking. In 2019, I moved to NZ and stay away from home so I had to "rolling into the kitchen" to have something to eat. (of course not rolling in to the deep like Adele :D ).

Firstly, cooking is something I must to do, but luckily gradually it becomes my hobby. The feeling of stuffed and rolled the dough, then watching your bread in the oven as well as its seductive aroma when it’s finished. Honestly, for me it is a feeling of happiness and joy.

I agree that in order to cook well or make good cakes, it actually need ingenuity and natural talent. What happen if unluckily that you do not have this “catalyst”? It totally is okay. The most thing play a paramount importance is your own determination, perseverance and desire – about being able to make delicious food, beautiful cakes for your family and friends. And if I can do it, you can too, that's for sure.

Stay gorgeous and eat cake !
